📹 How to add your videos

For YouTube: copy the embed URL (youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID) and paste as <iframe src="..."> inside any .video-embed div.

For local files: upload your .mp4 to public/videos/ and use <video controls src="videos/yourfile.mp4">.

You can add or remove .video-card blocks freely — the grid adjusts automatically.
